Biltong recipe

Biltong is a traditional food originating from South Africa. It originally came to be as a way to preserve meat when it was in abundance. Today it is a popular snack enjoyed by many South Africans.

The recipe for biltong is a simple as salt and vinegar with spices of your choice. Traditionally black pepper and coriander are used. But it really is a matter of taste.

The great thing about biltong is that one can experiment with seasonings and types of meat, creating something unique which you can enjoy and share with friends and family.

Ingredients required:

  • Meat of yuor choice
  • Salt
  • Vinegar
  • Your favourite seasonings

Steps:

  1. Slice meat into strips roughly 4cm thick
  2. Sprinkle the meat with a coating of salt and seasoning
  3. Allow to rest for approximately 2 hours
  4. Soak seasoned meet in vinegar for atleast four hours
  5. Hang marinated meat in a dark well ventilated space untli it is dry
  6. Eat and enjoy!

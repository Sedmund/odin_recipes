Bread recipe

Bread is known as the mother of all foods, it ruly is a versatile food, and mankind would not be what it is today without glorious bread.

Bread, like beer is very simple to make, in it's simplest form. Recipe's can be as complex or as simple as one can imagine. But like beer we require grain water and yeast to make bread. Oh and that the most important ingredient. Patience.

Ingredients required:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 sachet of bakers yeast

Steps:

  1. Combine flour and salt
  2. In a bowl combine the yeast with a little warm water. This will activate the yeast. Checking if it is still viable.
  3. Once the yeast has started to fizz, add it to the flour.
  4. Combine to make a ball.
  5. Knead the dough ball for 5 minutes.
  6. Cover and leave to rest in a warm place for atleast 1 hour.
  7. Once risen, knead out aggain and place in a container for proofing.
  8. Allow the dough to rise in the proofing container for atleast 2 hours.
  9. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius.
  10. Gently roll out the dough onto a baking tray and bake for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
  11. Enjoy!

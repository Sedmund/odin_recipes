Beer recipe

Beer is a great beverage consumed by humans for thousands of years.

There are many recipes to make beer and many versions of beer to be made. People have traditionally used what was available to them in abundance to make beer.

There are really only three main ingredients in a beer. Water, yeat and malted wheat, or barley or any malted grain.

Ingredients required:

  • 25 litres of water. Preferrably clean spring water
  • 6kg of malted barley
  • 1 sachet of brewers yeast

Equipment required:

  • 25 litre pot
  • Gas hob (Electric stoves take way to long and consume far to much energy with todays electricity prices!)
  • Mashtun
  • thermometer
  • Fermentor

Steps:

  1. Get 20 litres of water boiling on your hob.
  2. Once the water has reached 75 degrees celsius
  3. Add the malted barley to the mashtun
  4. Add the warm water to the mashtun
  5. Let steep for 1 hour
  6. Clean and sterilize fermentor
  7. Pour the drained liquid from the mashtun into the fermentor
  8. Once the wort (liquid) has cooled to 20 degrees celsius, add the sachet of brewers yeast
  9. allow to ferment for atleast two weeks.
